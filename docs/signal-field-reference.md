# Signal Field Reference

> Source of truth: `js/modules/signals-schema.js`. If the schema changes, update this doc in the same commit.

This document describes every field on a signal record. It is written for two audiences:

- **Technical operators** — people who enter, edit, or review data via the admin UI, Excel import, or AI extraction.
- **Contributor-network participants** — domain experts (supply-chain analysts, packaging engineers, market researchers) who provide intelligence but may not be developers.

For each field you will find: type, allowed values (if enum), a one-line description, a concrete example using real chip names and ABF specifications, and which entry point(s) populate it.

---

## 1. Identity Fields

These fields identify *what* the signal is about — the company, the chip, and the human-readable title.

### `id`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` (auto-generated Firestore document ID) |
| **Description** | Unique identifier for this signal record. Generated by Firestore on creation; not set by users. |
| **Example** | `"aB3cD4eF5gH6iJ7kL8mN"` (20-character auto-ID) |
| **Entry points** | System (Firestore `addDoc`) — never manually entered |

---

### `title`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` (required) |
| **Description** | Human-readable headline summarizing the signal in one line. |
| **Example** | `"NVIDIA B200 採用 TSMC CoWoS-L 封裝，ABF 基板需求爆發"` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel import, AI extraction |

---

### `company_id`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` (required) |
| **Description** | Firestore document ID of the parent company in the `companies` collection. Links the signal to a company profile. |
| **Example** | `"nvidia"` (links to the NVIDIA company record) |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction (matched against known company list) |

---

### `company_name`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` (required) |
| **Description** | Display name of the company. Redundant with `company_id` but kept for readable sorting and filtering without a join. |
| **Example** | `"NVIDIA"` or `"台積電"` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

### `chip_name`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` (required) |
| **Description** | Name of the specific chip or SoC this signal concerns. |
| **Example** | `"B200"`, `"MI325X"`, `"Kirin 9020"` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

## 2. Classification Fields

These fields categorize the signal for filtering, sorting, and analysis.

### `stage`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `enum` (string) |
| **Allowed values** | `rumor` (傳聞), `announced` (已宣布), `sampling` (送樣), `design_win` (設計勝出), `pilot` (試產), `ramp` (爬產), `volume` (量產) |
| **Description** | Where the chip is in its product lifecycle, from early rumor through mass production. |
| **Default** | `rumor` (if missing or unrecognized value) |
| **Example** | `"volume"` — AMD MI325X is in mass production at TSMC |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

### `status`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `enum` (string) |
| **Allowed values** | `draft` (草稿), `watch` (觀察中), `verified` (已驗證), `downgraded` (降級), `invalidated` (已失效), `archived` (已封存) |
| **Description** | Editorial workflow state — how much confidence the editorial team has placed in this signal after review. Distinct from `stage`: a signal can be about a chip in `volume` stage but still have `draft` status if it has not been editorially reviewed. |
| **Default** | `draft` (if missing or unrecognized value) |
| **Example** | `"watch"` — a rumor about Intel Falcon Shores ABF requirements, pending supplier confirmation |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry (AI extraction always forces `draft` or `watch`, never `verified`) |

---

### `region`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `enum` (string) |
| **Allowed values** | `China`, `Taiwan`, `USA`, `Korea`, `Japan`, `Europe`, `Israel`, `Canada`, `Other`, `Global` |
| **Description** | Primary geographic region where the signal originates or has the strongest impact. |
| **Example** | `"Taiwan"` — TSMC CoWoS-L capacity expansion in Taichung |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

### `signal_type`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` (free text) |
| **Description** | Short label describing the nature of the signal (e.g., capacity expansion, design change, supply disruption). Not an enum — operators can use descriptive terms. |
| **Example** | `"CoWoS產能擴張"` or `"ABF基板轉單"` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

## 3. ABF Fields

These fields capture the advanced packaging and ABF (Ajinomoto Build-up Film) substrate details that are the core focus of this platform.

### `abf_size`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` |
| **Description** | Physical dimensions of the ABF substrate used by this chip, typically expressed as width x height in millimeters. |
| **Example** | `"77mm x 77mm"` — NVIDIA B200 uses a large-format ABF substrate to accommodate the chiplet die area |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

### `abf_layers`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `number` (positive integer, nullable) |
| **Description** | Number of build-up layers in the ABF substrate. Higher layer counts indicate more complex routing and higher value per substrate. |
| **Example** | `18` — a high-end AI accelerator may require 16-20 layers for dense signal routing |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

### `cowos_required`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `boolean` |
| **Description** | Whether this chip requires TSMC CoWoS (Chip-on-Wafer-on-Substrate) advanced packaging. `true` means the chip depends on CoWoS capacity. |
| **Example** | `true` — NVIDIA B100, B200, and GB200 all require CoWoS-L |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

### `hbm`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` |
| **Description** | Type and configuration of HBM (High Bandwidth Memory) used by this chip. |
| **Example** | `"HBM3E 8-Hi"` — NVIDIA B200 uses 8-high HBM3E stacks for 288 GB capacity |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

### `abf_demand_impact`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `enum` (string) |
| **Allowed values** | `low` (低), `medium` (中), `high` (高), `explosive` (爆發性) |
| **Description** | How much this signal affects overall ABF substrate demand. Used as the primary sort key in the signal list. |
| **Default** | `low` (if missing or unrecognized value) |
| **Example** | `"explosive"` — NVIDIA B200 ramp with 77mm x 77mm substrate at 18 layers creates massive ABF demand |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

### Additional ABF-related fields (not ABF-specific but packaging-related)

| Field | Type | Description | Example | Entry points |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `release_year` | `number` (nullable) | Target year of chip release or the event described. | `2025` for NVIDIA B200 | Manual, Excel, AI |
| `release_quarter` | `enum`: `Q1`, `Q2`, `Q3`, `Q4` or empty | Target quarter within the release year. | `"Q2"` for a mid-2025 tape-out | Manual, Excel, AI |
| `package_type` | `string` | Packaging technology used. | `"CoWoS-L"`, `"InFO"`, `"FCoB"` | Manual, Excel, AI |
| `expected_volume` | `string` | Expected production volume or capacity figure. | `"100K wafers/year"` or `"50K CoWoS/month"` | Manual, Excel, AI |

---

## 4. Confidence and Evidence Fields

These fields explain *why* the signal is believed and what evidence supports it.

### `confidence_score`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `number` (0-100) |
| **Description** | Numerical confidence level. 0 = no confidence, 100 = fully verified. Clamped to 0-100 by the schema. AI-extracted signals without explicit sources are capped at 60. |
| **Example** | `85` — multiple supplier sources confirm TSMC CoWoS-L expansion, but official announcement pending |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, AI extraction (auto-capped), Excel Import |

---

### `confidence_reason`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` |
| **Description** | Text explanation of why the confidence score is set at its current level — what is missing or uncertain. |
| **Example** | `"僅有供應鏈消息，TSMC 未正式確認；ABF 供應商未回應詢"` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, AI extraction |

---

### `evidence_summary`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` |
| **Description** | Summary of the evidence supporting this signal. Must cite concrete details from the source material, not abstract claims. |
| **Example** | `"Digitimes 報導 TSMC 已向南電訂購 18 層 ABF 基板用於 B200；供應鏈人士確認 CoWoS-L 產能擴張中"` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, AI extraction (required for confidence > 60), Excel Import |

---

### `conflicting_evidence`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` |
| **Description** | Any evidence that contradicts or casts doubt on this signal. |
| **Example** | `"Intel 官方 roadmap 顯示 Falcon Shores 推遲至 2026 H1，與之前 2025 Q4 送樣消息矛盾"` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, AI extraction, Excel Import |

---

### `sources`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `array of strings` |
| **Description** | List of source references — URLs, publication names, or document titles. |
| **Example** | `["https://www.digitimes.com/news/...", "TSMC Q1 2025 earnings call transcript"]` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction |

---

## 5. Lifecycle Timestamps

These fields track the signal's editorial lifecycle — when it was created, modified, verified, and when key attributes changed.

### `createdAt`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `Firestore server timestamp` (stored as ISO 8601 string after normalization) |
| **Description** | When this signal record was first created in Firestore. Set automatically; not user-editable. |
| **Example** | `"2025-03-15T08:30:00.000Z"` |
| **Entry points** | System (Firestore `serverTimestamp()`) |

---

### `updatedAt`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `Firestore server timestamp` (stored as ISO 8601 string after normalization) |
| **Description** | When this signal was last modified. Updated automatically on every `saveSignal` call. |
| **Example** | `"2025-04-20T14:22:00.000Z"` |
| **Entry points** | System (Firestore `serverTimestamp()`) |

---

### `last_verified_at`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` (ISO 8601 date) |
| **Description** | Date when this signal was most recently verified against a primary source. |
| **Example** | `"2025-04-18"` — analyst confirmed CoWoS-L capacity figure during supplier visit |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry |

---

### `last_status_changed_at`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` (ISO 8601 date) or `null` |
| **Description** | Auto-stamped when `status` changes (e.g., `draft` → `watch`). Set by `saveSignal`, not by users. |
| **Example** | `"2025-04-19T10:00:00.000Z"` — status changed from draft to watch after editor review |
| **Entry points** | System (auto-stamped by `saveSignal`) |

---

### `last_confidence_changed_at`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` (ISO 8601 date) or `null` |
| **Description** | Auto-stamped when `confidence_score` changes. Set by `saveSignal`, not by users. |
| **Example** | `"2025-04-20T09:15:00.000Z"` — confidence raised from 40 to 75 after new source confirmation |
| **Entry points** | System (auto-stamped by `saveSignal`) |

---

## 6. AI Metadata Fields

These fields are populated only when a signal originates from or is processed by the AI extraction pipeline.

### `ai_generated`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `boolean` |
| **Description** | `true` if this signal was created or processed by the AI extraction pipeline. |
| **Example** | `true` — signal was extracted from a news article by DeepSeek |
| **Entry points** | AI extraction (`normalizeAiCandidates`) |

---

### `ai_model`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` |
| **Description** | Name of the AI model used for extraction (e.g., the DeepSeek model configured in admin settings). |
| **Example** | `"deepseek-v4-flash"` |
| **Entry points** | AI extraction |

---

### `ai_extracted_at`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` (ISO 8601 date) |
| **Description** | Timestamp when the AI extraction ran and produced this signal. |
| **Example** | `"2025-05-10T03:45:00.000Z"` |
| **Entry points** | AI extraction |

---

### `source_type`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` |
| **Description** | Indicates the provenance of the signal. Set to `"ai_extracted"` when coming from the AI pipeline. |
| **Example** | `"ai_extracted"` or `"manual_entry"` or `"excel_import"` |
| **Entry points** | AI extraction, Manual entry, Excel Import |

---

## 7. Categorization Arrays

These fields provide additional filtering and grouping dimensions.

### `tags`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `array of strings` |
| **Description** | Free-form tags for categorization. Converted from comma-separated strings if provided as text. |
| **Example** | `["CoWoS", "ABF載板", "AI加速器", "先进封装"]` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction (split from comma-separated string) |

---

### `source_regions`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `array of strings` |
| **Description** | Geographic regions mentioned in the source material for this signal. May differ from `region` (which is the primary region). |
| **Example** | `["Taiwan", "Japan"]` — signal primarily about Taiwan, but sources mention Japan's Ajinomoto ABF supply |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction (split from comma-separated string) |

---

### `impact_scope`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `array of strings` |
| **Description** | Scope of impact — which parts of the supply chain or market segments are affected. |
| **Example** | `["ABF基板供應商", "CoWoS產能", "HBM供應鏈"]` — the signal impacts ABF substrate suppliers, CoWoS capacity, and HBM supply chain |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import, AI extraction (split from comma-separated string) |

---

## 8. Notes and Verification Fields

These fields support editorial workflow — notes for future editors and records of who verified what.

### `notes`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` |
| **Description** | Free-form editorial notes about this signal — context, follow-up actions, or related signals. |
| **Example** | `"需追蹤南電下一季法說會對 ABF 產能的指引；與 signal #aB3cD4 相關（同為 B200 供應鏈）"` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry, Excel Import |

---

### `verification_note`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` |
| **Description** | Specific note left by the person who last verified this signal — what they checked and how. |
| **Example** | `"已交叉比對 TSMC 法說會紀錄與供應商採購單，CoWoS-L 擴產計劃確認"` |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry |

---

### `last_verified_by`

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | `string` |
| **Description** | Name or identifier of the person who last verified this signal. |
| **Example** | `"張分析師"` or `"contributor-tw-003"` (for contributor-network participants) |
| **Entry points** | Manual entry |
